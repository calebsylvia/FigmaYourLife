Benimaru Shinmon is my name. I am also known as the King of Destruction due to my immense powers. I am a second and third generation pyrokinetic. I was an orphan growing up until I was adopted by Hibachi, he is the one that taught me everything I know. Becoming so strong brought me up into becoming the Captain of Special Fire Force Company 7. I later retired to take care and protect my beloved residence, Asakusa. I will do everything to make sure my people are okay and one day we will all celebrate together.
